## 保活

- 先运行保活脚本，防止被回收了

可以配合保活脚本【**[开源脚本](https://github.com/spiritLHLS/Oracle-server-keep-alive-script)**】：
```
bash <(wget -qO- --no-check-certificate https://gitlab.com/spiritysdx/Oracle-server-keep-alive-script/-/raw/main/oalive.sh)
```
